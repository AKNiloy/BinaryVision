About

The team was founded by several students from the dept. of mechatronics Engineering, MTE, RUET in 2018.
The team took part in the Tata makerthon challange organized by ESAB for the IITB Techfest Bangladesh round.
Being the national champion, the team was the one of the finalist in the IIT Techfest final round.
After that, the team took part in several competition and did several research collaboration with other universitites.
